### What do I do?
I work as a **Data Scientist** at [GreenPlace](https://greenplace.io). Our goal is to help SME on their decarbonisation journey of their real estate assets in the UK. 

My job is to do anything with data – I've built some internal data tools, designed (spatial) data models, produced analyses, dashboards and data acquisition pipelines. 

My economics background puts me in a position of market modelling but I strive to solve any challenge that I encounter.

### Did someone say economics?
Yes! I studied [Economics & Business Economics](https://www.eur.nl/en/bachelor/international-bachelor-economics-and-business-economics) at the Erasmus University in Rotterdam and I finished my MSc in [Business Analytics](https://www.bath.ac.uk/courses/postgraduate-2026/taught-postgraduate-courses/msc-business-analytics/) at the University of Bath.

I'm passionate about new economic thinking and I'm currently deep-diving into the Complexity Economics. Perhaps my future steps will lead towards this topic.

Other than that, you'll certainly find me doing any sport (mainly hockey, cycling or hiking the least frequent trails) or reading a nerdy book. My favourites are about nature, reportage from conflict zones or mathematics.
